Project Manager

AMSTERDAM | ENGINEERING | UNIVERSITY DEGREE

At Kinly, we think working together is the most exciting thing in the world. We are on a fast and exciting journey to introduce this message to every company and every professional in the world. Become part of this journey and join us as Project Manager. We promise you won’t regret it. Job pros and cons

+ You get to work for leading customers, events and brands.
+ You are responsible for the successful implementation and delivery of our high-profile installation projects throughout the country.
+ You are in the heart of our business, which means dealing with all layers of our organization.
+ You monitor progress, quality, safety, budget and customer satisfaction.
+ No two days are the same - and that’s awesome. On Monday you may be present at a construction site. On Tuesday you may sit around the table with a CEO. On Wednesday you may be present at our headquarters for a meeting, some administration or a social drink with your colleagues. The rest of the week? Fill it in yourself.
- Having a few days off is not nearly as much fun as it was before, because you miss your colleagues.

Your experience and skills

· You have a degree (hbo or wo), for example in Project Management/Prince II.
· You have a proven track record as Project Manager.
· You have the ambition and competence to lead our internal and external project staff.
· You have affinity with AV and IT.
· You are an excellent communicator. And yes, you are also pragmatic and punctual.

This is Kinly

Hire on attitude and train on competence, that is our credo. We offer you an ambitious environment, a great work atmosphere and enough growth opportunities to make your time at Kinly valuable. The terms of employment are also excellent, such as a competitive salary and more than enough vacation days (but only if you promise to return after each holiday).
